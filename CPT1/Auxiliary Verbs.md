There are 5 auxiliary verbs:

1. 要
2. 不用
3. 会
4. 能
5. 可以

1 - 2 are related
3 - 5 are related

---
# 要

1. *strong desire* of "want"
2. "need" or "should" or "must"

>[!tip]
>Use `想` to express *soft desire* of "want". 
>See [[Usage of 想]]
## Negation

1. Negation of "want": `不想`
2. Negation of "need" or "should" or "must": `不用+Verb`

>[!note] Use `想不想` for interrogative of strong desire

## Example

"need" or "should"

>这个词==要==翻译吗？
>——这个词很容易，==不用==翻译。
>Should this word be translated?
>This word is easy, no need to translate.

"want"

>你==要==这本书吗？
>——我==不想==这本书。

# 会

to know how to (skill acquired through practice)
## Negation

`不会` or `不太会`

> [!NOTE] Keyword for English translation
> When translating into English write "know how to...".

>我==不太会==做中国菜。
>I really don't know how to cook Chinese dish.

---

# 能

1. can be able to
2. regain of an ability or restart of something
3. ability of skill up to a certain degree

> [!NOTE] Keyword for English translation
> When translating write "can...".
## Negation

`不能`

## Examples


Regain of ability

>他病好了，能上课。
>He has recovered from being ill, and can attend classes.

Degree of skill

>你能游一百米吗？
>——我不能。
>Can you swim 100 meters?
>I can't.



---

# 可以

Permission seeking or permission granted

## Negation

`不能`

## Examples

>我们==可以从==这儿起吗？
>Can we start from here?
>Also see [[Prepositions#从]]


>那个大操场我们可以打篮球吗？
>——==不能==打篮球，==只能==踢足球。
>Can we play basketball on that big playground?
>Cannot play basketball, can only play football.
