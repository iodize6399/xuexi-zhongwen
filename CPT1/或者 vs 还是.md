# 还是

- Use to ask which options in a question.
- Also called as `choice-type questions`
- Always results in a question.

>你去公园==还是==去学校？
>Do you go to the park or to the school?
>你去==还是==他去?
>Will you or he go?
>你去教室==还是==去图书馆?
>Will you go the school or library?

>电影厅在楼上==还是==在楼下？
>Movie hall is at upstairs or downstairs?
	在 is acting as a verb of location

# 或者

Use to list possibilities/options in a declarative statement.

>我去公园==或者==去学校。
>I go to the park or the school.

>[!tip] Using `吗` with 或者
>End the statement with `吗` to convert into choice question

>[!note]
>Always repeat the verb after 还是 and 或者
