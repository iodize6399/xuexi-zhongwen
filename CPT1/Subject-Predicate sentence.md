- `Topic-Comment` or `Double Subject` sentence

>[!note] 
>Possessive word `的` is dropped

>==他身体==很好。
>His health is good.

>==我们学校==留学生很多。
>Our school has many international students.

>==中国菜==很好吃。
>Chinese food is delicious.
