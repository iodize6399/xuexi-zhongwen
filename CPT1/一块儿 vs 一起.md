- They both mean "together" are substitutable if used with `和`.

>安娜==和==麦柯昌==一起==去操场。
>Anna and Mike, together go to stadium.
>哈利==和==小王常==一块儿==练习拼音。
>Hali and Xiao Wang, often practice pinyin together.

>[!caution]
>it will wrong to substitute `一起` with `一块儿` when used with `跟`.
>See [[Prepositions#跟]]
