## Interrogative pronouns

### 多少

- how many (expecting more than 10)
- Use of measure word between `多少` and noun is optional.

>你们班有==多少==学生？
>==How many== students are there in your class?
>你们学校有==多少==外国老师？
>==How many== foreign teachers does your school have?

- When asking for price generally 多少 is preferred and mandatory to suffix with 钱

>这本中文书==多少钱==？
>How much does this Chinese book costs?
### 几

#### General `what`

- (expecting less than 10) or what (depending on context)
- Use of measure word between `几` and noun is mandatory.

>他有==几本==中文书？
>==How many== Chinese books does he have?

#### to ask about time

>现在==几点==钟？
>What's the time now?

Also see [[#什么时候]]

## 什么时候

>==什么时候==老师开设上课？
>When does the teacher start the classes?

## 吗 - Interrogative Particle

### Simple yes-no

>上午你练习拼音==马==？
>Do you practice pinyin in the morning?

### Soliciting approval (Only expecting "yes")

End the question with `...好吗？`

>我们一起去图书馆，==好吗==？
>Shall we go to the library together?

>我问问题，你回答，==好吗==？
>I shall ask the question, and you answer, okay?

## Rhetorical question using `呢`

- Asks a rhetorical question

>我叫麦克，你==呢==？
>My name is Mike, what about you?

## 怎么样

"how"
### To ask condition of a noun

`N + 怎么样 ？`

>这件毛衣==怎么样==？
>How is this sweater?

### To ask about quality of the verb

> 他翻译句子翻译得==怎么样==？
> How does he translates sentences?

## Affirmative-negative

`Adj. + 不 + Adj.`
### To ask/confirm about quality of the verb

- put the complement in affirmative-negative form.

> 他打篮球打得很==好不好==？
> Does he play basketball well or not?
