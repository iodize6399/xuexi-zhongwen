- main part of the sentence is a verb (action word)
- if subject does *something*, then, that *something* (Object) comes after the verb.

>他们看。
>He watches.
>谢力做中国菜。
>Shelly cooks Chinese food.

# Negation

Write `不` before the verb.

>我不看杂志，我看画报。
