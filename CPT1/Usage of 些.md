- Indicates indefinite quantity
- it is a measure word

Prefix it after `这` and `那` to make "these" and "those" respectively.
>==这些==安娜的蓝色的钢笔。
>==These== are Anna's blue pens.

Can only be prefixed with numeral `一`, by replacing the measure word of the noun to make the noun as "some" of the noun.
>我有==一些==红色的中文杂志。
>I have ==some== red Chinese magazines.
