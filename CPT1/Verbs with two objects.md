# Structure

`Subject + Verb + Person (Indirect Object) + Thing (Direct Object)`
## 给

==To give==

`Sender/Sub + 给 + Recipient (Animate) + Verb + Obj`

>他给我喝水。
>He gives me water to drink.

`Sender/Sub + 给 + Recipient (Animate) + Obj (Inanimate)`

>他给我一张电影票。
>He gives me a movie ticket.

## 教

==To teach==

`Who + 教 + To Whom (Animate) + What (Inanimate)`

>王老师教我们汉语。
>Teacher Wang teaches us Chinese language.


## Similarly for 问, 借, 还 and 找

>我==问==他三个问题。
>I ask him 3 questions.

>安娜==还==她弟弟笔。
>Anna returns the pen to her younger brother.

>我==借==好朋友一些钱。
>I borrow some money from my best friend.

>你能==找==我十块钱吗？
>Can you give me ten Yuan change?
>

>[!tip] Use `从...借` to mean "borrow"
>Sub. + 从 (cóng) + Person + 借 + Thing
>>我==从==老师==借==书。
>>I borrow a book from the teacher.
