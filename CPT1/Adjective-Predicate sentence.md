# Use `很`

- main part of the sentence is an adjective

>[!caution]
>Donot place `是` before an adjective.

>这本词典很好。
>This dictionary is very good.
>我们的教室很干净。
>Our classroom is very neat.

>[!tip] Compare by removing `很`
>	男学生多，女学生少。
>	There are more male students as compared to female students, who are few.

# Negation

Negation of sentences with an adjective as its predicate

place `不` before the adjective

>[!danger]
>Don't use `很` anywhere in the sentence when negating an adjective.

# Affirmative-negative question

- put together affirmative and negative forms of the verb (or adjective)

>[!note] Affirmative-negative for `有`
>Use `有没有`

# Using demonstrative pronoun as an attributive with the adjective

- Add the measure word when demonstrative pronouns is used as an attribute with the adjective.

>==这本==书很好。
>This book is very good.
>==那个==生词难不难？
>Is that new word difficult or not?
