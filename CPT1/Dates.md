# Placement of [[Measure Words]]

Don't use measure words for Date (号/日) and Year (年)
Use measure word 个 for Month (月) and Week (星期)

❌ Date/Year → **No 个**  
✅ Month/Week → **Use 个**

>一==年==有十二==个月==
>A year has 12 months.
>一==个星==期有七==天==
>A week has 7 days.

>[!tip] Remove 些 when specific numeral are specified.
>这七天是:...
>==These== 7 days are...
>这十二个月是:...
>==These== 12 months are...

# Expressing a calendar year

Say the numbers in telephone style (speak each number)

# Expressing names of months

Put the number of the specific month in front of `月`

>[!note]
>February is `二月`
>See [[两 vs 二#Before Month]]

# Expressing dates

Like expressing months, put the number in front of `日`

>[!caution]
>Use `日` instead of `号` when writing dates. Use `号` in spoken Chinese only.

# Expressing names of days

Prefix `星期` then the numeral of the weekday.

>[!note]
>Tuesday is `星期二`
>See [[两 vs 二#For Day]]
# Order of writing Dates

`Year + Month + Date + (Day) + (Time-of-Day) + Hour + Minute`
