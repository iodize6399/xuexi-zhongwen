Modifier used before verb or adjective which is called the central word.

Eg. 一起, 常, 努, 力, 都, 也

>[!note]
>repeat `常` as `常常` in discourse but negation is `不常`, never `不常常`
# Negation

`S + 不 + Adv. + Verb + Obj.`

# Patterns

## All...too

`也` comes before `都`
>他们是学生，你们==也都==是学生。
>She is a student, they are ==all== students ==too==.

## Only have/has

`只` come before `有`
>你们班==只有==男同学吗？
>Does you class ==only have== male students?

## Also have/has

`也` comes before `有`
>不，我们班==也有==女同学。
>No, our class ==also have== female students.

>你==常和*==谁一起去图书馆、阅览室？
>With whom do you often go to the library and reading room?
>* Can also use [[Prepositions#跟|跟]]

## Other patterns

>你们的宿舍==也在==学校里边吗？
>Is your hostel also inside the school?

>星期日我==也常==进城买东西。
>I also often go to the downtown to buy things on Sunday.
# Weird sentences

>==这儿有==中文杂志，==也有==外文杂志。
>==Here, there are== Chinese magazines, ==as well as== foreign magazines.
