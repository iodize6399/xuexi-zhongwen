They are **used as verbs**.

1. 在
2. 有
3. 是

| Verb        | Core Meaning      | Word Order                | Answers the Question    | Meta                              | Core                                            | Hindi               | Keyword           | Negation |
| :---------- | :---------------- | :------------------------ | :---------------------- | :-------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- | :------------------ | :---------------- | :------- |
| **在 (zài)** | To be located at  | **Thing** + 在 + **Place** | Where is (the Thing)?   | Where to find (that Thing)        | State its location                              | वो subject है कहां? | is/are at         | 不 + 在    |
| **有 (yǒu)** | To have; There is | **Place** + 有 + **Thing** | What is in (the Place)? | What things exists at (the Place) | List its contents. Usually deals with quantity. | वो जगह पर है क्या?  | There is/are      | 没 + 有    |
| **是 (shì)** | To be (identity)  | **Place** + 是 + **Thing** | What is (the Place)?    | What fills (that Place) "Scene"   | State its identity. It categories or labels.    | वो जगह है क्या?     | ____ is/are ____. | 不 + 是    |
- Start with location, you are setting the scene. Follow with **是** tells **what fills that scene**.
- Start with subject/thing, you have introduced a subject/thing. follow with **在** tells **where to find that subject**.

Use 是 when things are most specific:
1. name of the location is given
2. adjective of that location is described

>[!tip] Repeat 在 when used with 还是
>电影厅==在楼上==还是==在楼下==？

>[!warning]
>When using 在 as a verb (location word) do not end it with a verb phrase, or else, it will become a preposition.

>[!caution] 在 as a preposition
>Always ends with an action word/verb. See [[Prepositions]]
>
>你在清华大学==学习==汉语。
>I ==study== at Qing Hua University.


## Examples

The park is ==in== front of my home.
公园==在==我家前边。

==There is a== park in front of my home.
我家前边==有==一个公园。

In front of my home ==is, Beihai Park==.
我家前边==是==北海公园。

## Weird Sentences

1. 里边的屋子大不大？Is the room which is inside, big or not?
2. 礼堂西边是什么==地方==？What is to the west of the auditorium?
3. 东边的是教学楼。The east one is the teaching building.
4. 我的学校离==城里==不近。`Notice how 里 is used after 离 with 成 to convert it into a location`
