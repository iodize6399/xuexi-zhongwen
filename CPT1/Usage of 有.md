# Possession

when taken as predicate verb, it denotes possession of something

>他有中文画报。
>He has Chinese pictorial.

# Existence or presence

>这儿有几把椅子？
>how many chairs are there, here?
>宿舍有几个书架？
>How many bookshelves are there in the hostel?

# Negation

Negate 有 using 没

>他没有中文画报。
>He does not have a Chinese pictorial.
