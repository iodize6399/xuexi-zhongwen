>[!note] 
>The `是` (as a verb) is dropped

> 我北京人。
> I'm from Beijing.

>一件毛衣二十四块钱。
>A sweater costs 24 Yuan.

>[!tip] Negate using `不是`
>Put `不是` before the predicate to negate.

>现在不是四点钟。
>Currently, it is not 4 o'clock.
